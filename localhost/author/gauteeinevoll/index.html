About Gaute Einevoll

I am a professor at the Department of Physics, University of Oslo and at the Department of Mathematical Sciences, Norwegian University of Life Sciences at Ås. My research focuses on theoretical neurophysics/computational neuroscience/neuroinformatics. At Ås I teach courses both in computational neuroscience (FYS388) and biological physics (FYS381).
